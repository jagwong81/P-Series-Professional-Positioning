# P Series – Professional Positioning

The P Series focuses on professional positioning, project coordination experience, role definition, and career transition observations.

While the L Series explores laboratory systems, operational continuity, and coordination behavior, the P Series addresses a different question:

**What role did I actually play in those projects, and where do I fit within project delivery environments today?**

This series is not intended to redefine past experience. Its purpose is to clarify responsibilities, project interfaces, coordination activities, and lessons learned through laboratory and multidisciplinary project work.
